iCite database snapshots are stored here within versioned subfolders (yyyy-mm) 
as `icite_metadata.zip` files. 

Each is about 10GB, so they are not git-controlled. 

Download the latest iCite snapshot from NIH FigShare: 
https://nih.figshare.com/collections/iCite_Database_Snapshots_NIH_Open_Citation_Collection_/4586573
